An admiral of the French Navy, François was famous for bringing the remains of Napoleon from Saint Helena to France, as well as a talented artist, with 35 known watercolours. He married Princess Francisca of Brazil, daughter of Emperor Pedro I and sister of Emperor Pedro II.The dowry received by François upon the marriage became the Brazilian city of Joinville.François and Francisca's grandson Jean went on to become the Orléanist claimant to the extinct French throne, a claim passed on to his son, grandson and now great-grandson Jean, Count of Paris, current Orléanist claimant to the French crown.Show lessRead moreWikipediaDiscover this historical figure6 itemsOrganize byTranslate with GoogleGoogle apps
